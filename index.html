The Cow Goes Moo

Ever heard this saying, but never knew what it meant? Well it's time to clear that confusion up. This phrase is used to tell us the vital information about cows and what sound they make (in case you were wondering, they "moo"). It's hard to believe without hearing it for yourself, so I recommend you go to your nearest farm and give it a listen!
